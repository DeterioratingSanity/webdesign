About Us:
The Doggone Good Dog Walking Service was founded in 2021 by John, a native of the Golden Isles with a love for everything canine (Except the teeth!). In the last year, Doggone Good has hired walkers who manage the island neighborhood by neighborhood, while maintaining training, boarding, and grooming facilities out of the main building. Pet dietitians and trainers supervise the care of, on average, 107 dogs each day, as well as conducting weekly seminars to clients. Further, John personally leads a monthly Orientation Club, taking new clients and canines on a tour of the premises.
